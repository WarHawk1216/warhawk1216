About Me

Hello! My name is Manraj Brar and I am an engineering student at The University of British Columbia Vancouver. I graduated from Pitt Meadows Secondary and have lived in Pitt Meadows my entire life. I am interested in programming, CAD, aviation, cars, philosophy, basketball, and lifting weights.


I've created this website to track whatever progress I make in any subject area I'm invested in and to organize my thoughts, opinions, ideas, etc. (not that they're especially important or worth noting). I hope to, one day, work in aerospace engineering or any mechanically oriented field of work in which I can solve problems with novel solutions.


Currently, I am working on a few projects within different spheres of my interest, all of which I'll update on either the Blog page or the Portfolio page, but I'll summarize them here as well. The one which is most apparent is this website which I've started as a summer project, since I lack sufficient funds I'm using GitHub Pages. Some other things I'm working on as of writing this are a four cylinder engine in SolidWorks, turbocharging and tuning my 8th Generation Honda Civic, and recreating a Plasma Toroid (as shown by BackMacSci). Of course, each of these projects take a fair amount of time, so I may complete some now or later — right now (as may be kind of obvious) I'm invested in completing this site and working on the Civic.


Anyways, anything I post onto this site isn't to be taken so seriously and is just another thing I wanted to do. If you think I could fix anything on here or have any suggestions please feel free to contact me! I'm always up for improvement.
